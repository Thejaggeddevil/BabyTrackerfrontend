# 👶 Millionaire Baby Club

A **smart parenting companion app** built for new parents. From the day your baby arrives, this app guides you through every milestone, activity, and decision — with a full development roadmap designed to give your child the best possible start.

## ✨ Features

- 🗺️ **Full Child Development Roadmap** — stage-by-stage guidance from newborn to toddler
- 🎯 **Age-wise Activity Recommendations** — curated activities suitable for your baby's current age
- ✅ **Milestone Tracker** — log and monitor your baby's physical, cognitive, and social progress
- 💡 **Smart Guidance System** — "If X happens, do Y" — actionable advice for common parenting situations
- 📊 **Baby Growth Data** — track weight, height, and health checkups
- 🔔 **Reminders** — vaccination schedules, feeding times, and doctor visits
- 🧡 **Parent-friendly UI** — calm, clean design for sleep-deprived parents

## 💛 Who Is This For?

This app is built for **new and expecting parents** who want a structured, reliable companion to navigate the beautiful (and sometimes overwhelming) journey of raising a baby. Whether it's your first child or your third, Millionaire Baby Club keeps you informed, prepared, and confident.
